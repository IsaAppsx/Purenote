Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 7, 2026
At PureNote, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains our "Offline-First" philosophy and how we protect your data.

1. Data Collection Philosophy

PureNote is designed to be a private, secure, and offline-first note-taking application.

  • No Account Required: You do not need to create an account to use the app.
  • No Data Harvesting: We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data.
  • No Analytics: We do not use third-party tracking or analytics services to monitor your usage.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not have access to, nor do we collect:

  • Your notes, titles, or attachments (photos, audio recordings).
  • Your master PIN, passwords, or recovery keys.
  • Your device identifiers or location data.
  • Any metadata associated with your notes.

3. Data Storage and Encryption

All data you create within PureNote is stored locally on your device.

  • Local Storage: Your notes and settings are stored in a local database (Isar) on your device's internal storage.
  • Encryption: Notes marked as "Locked" are encrypted using industry-standard AES-256-GCM encryption. The encryption keys are managed by your device's secure hardware (Keystore/Keychain).
  • Media: Photos and voice recordings are stored in a private directory on your device and are not accessible by other applications.

4. Backups and Cloud Synchronization

PureNote provides features for you to back up your data, but you maintain full control over where that data goes.

  • Local Backups: If you create a backup file, it is saved to a location you choose on your device or external storage.
  • Cloud Sync (Optional): If you enable synchronization features (e.g., Google Drive), your data is uploaded directly from your device to your private cloud storage. We never see or handle this data. It is subject to the privacy policy of the cloud provider you choose.

5. Device Permissions

PureNote may request the following permissions to provide specific features:

  • Microphone: Required for voice notes and dictation.
  • Storage/Media: Required to save attachments and export backups.
  • Biometrics: Required if you choose to use Fingerprint or Face ID to unlock your Vault.
  • Notifications: Required for note reminders.

6. Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any information, PureNote is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. However, our core philosophy of "No Data Collection" will remain. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at: support@yourdomain.com

🔒 Security Reminder: As PureNote is a local-only tool, your data is as secure as your device. We recommend using a strong master PIN and keeping your recovery key in a safe place.
