Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 7, 2026
PureNote respects your privacy. PureNote is an offline-first note-taking application designed to keep your personal data stored locally on your device and under your control.

1. Privacy Philosophy

PureNote is designed as a private, secure, and offline-first notebook application. We minimize data collection and do not operate servers that store your personal notes.

  • No Account Required: You can use PureNote without creating an account.
  • No Data Collection: We do not collect, sell, or share your notes or personal content.
  • No Tracking: PureNote does not use advertising trackers or third-party analytics services.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

PureNote does not have access to or collect:

  • Your notes, journals, checklists, or personal writings.
  • Your photos, voice recordings, or attached files.
  • Your PIN, passwords, biometric data, or recovery information.
  • Your location or device identifiers.

3. Local Storage and Protection

All content created in PureNote is stored locally on your device. Your notes are not uploaded to PureNote servers.

  • Local Storage: Notes, settings, and application data are stored on your device.
  • Secure Vault: Notes marked as protected are secured using encryption and device security features.
  • Media: Photos and voice recordings are stored locally and are not sent to PureNote.

4. Backup and Export

PureNote provides local backup and export features while keeping control of your data with you.

  • Local Backup: Backup files are created locally and saved to a location selected by you.
  • Export: You can export notes as PDF, Markdown, HTML, or JSON files.
  • Export Files: Exported files are standard files managed by the user and may not have the same protection as Secure Vault notes.
  • User Responsibility: Users are responsible for protecting exported files and keeping important backups.

5. Data Loss Responsibility

Because PureNote stores data locally on your device, users should maintain backups of important information.

PureNote cannot guarantee recovery of data lost due to accidental deletion, device damage, device reset, operating system problems, or application removal.

6. Device Permissions

  • Microphone: Used for voice recording and speech-to-text features.
  • Photos and Media: Used when adding attachments or exporting notes.
  • Biometrics: Used only when you enable fingerprint or Face ID protection.
  • Notifications: Used for reminders when enabled.

7. Children's Privacy

PureNote does not knowingly collect personal information from children or users. All user-created data remains on the user's device.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions , please contact us at isaapps.support@protonmail.com.

🔒 Security Reminder: Your data is stored locally on your device. Keep your device secure and protect your recovery information.
